Overall Layout

Full-width section using a Google Maps satellite background (blurred or darkened slightly).

Apply a dark blue gradient overlay from left to right:

Gradient direction: 90deg (left → right)

Colors: #0A1A3D at 0%, fading to rgba(10,26,61,0.4) at 60%, then transparent at 100%.

The left 35% of the screen is darker (solid navy overlay) for text visibility.

The remaining right area (with the map visible) hosts interactive cards and control buttons.

Club Cards (Bottom Overlay)

Located at the bottom center of the map area.

Cards are horizontally aligned (carousel-like layout).

Each card displays info for a local club.

Slight elevation and shadow effect to stand out against the map.

Card Style:

Shape: Rounded rectangle with border-radius: 16px

Background: rgba(10, 26, 61, 0.8) gradient overlay

Padding: 20px 24px

Margin between cards: 24px

Box-shadow: 0px 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.3)

Card Content:

Each card includes:

Club icon (left side):

Circle with white border and inner yellow logo (like a flying bird).

Icon size: 60px × 60px

Border: 2px solid white

Club title:

Example: Clubs 01 FSTH

Font size: 20–22px, weight: 700, color: #FFFFFF

Address:

Example: 62 Via A, Bokidadan AL Hoceima

Font size: 14–16px, color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.8)

Optional badge: a small yellow circle in the corner with a number (23).

Expanded Club Card (on selection)

When a card is clicked or active:

It expands slightly (scale 1.05).

Background changes to lighter gradient:
linear-gradient(180deg, rgba(10,26,61,0.9) 0%, rgba(255,255,255,0.1) 100%)

Displays an image thumbnail of the location at the bottom-left corner.

Includes a “Get More” button:

Style: Rounded (border-radius: 8px)

Background: linear-gradient(90deg, #F8B500 0%, #FFDA7A 100%)

Text: “Get More”

Font: 16px, bold, white

Padding: 10px 20px

Summary

A full-width interactive section with a map background and dark blue gradient overlay.
On the left, a vertical list of Moroccan cities with the current city highlighted.
Centered title and subtitle at the top.
Club cards appear over the map at the bottom with hover/active animation and a “Get More” button.
Map control buttons are floating on the right side.
